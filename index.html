About Us

Sweet Snowball is dedicated to serving the finest quality ice-cream treats made with passion and care. Our commitment is to delight your taste buds with premium flavors crafted from the best ingredients.

Founded in 2022 by Mrs. Seema Giri, Sweet Snowball has been serving delightful ice cream flavors that bring joy to people of all ages. With a focus on quality and freshness, our brand has become a favorite destination for ice cream lovers.

Our Mission

To bring joy and wellness to our customers through natural ice-cream flavours, made with real ingredients and sustainable practices. Becoming the most loved destination for natural ice cream across the region.

Why Sweet Snowball?

We believe in quality, creativity, and community. Every scoop is a result of our dedication to providing a premium experience that leaves you wanting more.
